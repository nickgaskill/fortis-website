Our story

Strong. Steadfast. Built to last.

The name comes first. Fortis is Latin — strong, steadfast, built to last. We didn't pick it because it sounds good. We picked it because it's a promise, and every project we take on has to live up to it.

Fortis Contracting Group is a locally owned construction company based in Austin, Texas. We specialize in interior renovations, outdoor living, and pools — and we build home additions, sunrooms, and ground-up projects across Austin and the surrounding cities. What makes us different is simple: one company runs your whole project. Every trade, one point of contact, a line-item estimate before work begins, and steady communication until the final walkthrough.

Meet the owner

Nick Gaskill

Fortis is owned and led by Nick Gaskill. Nick is on your project from the first walkthrough to the last — he sets the estimate, runs the crews, and answers the phone when you call. When you hire Fortis, you're not handed off to a rotating cast of project managers. You get the person whose name is on the company.

Consultations are always free, and they always start the same way: we listen first.
