Portfolio · Georgetown, TX

Dovetail Lane

A custom Hill Country estate — stone and stucco outside, wide-open living inside, and a resort-style backyard.

The project

Every room, one standard

A stone-and-stucco elevation with a barrel-tile roof opens onto a paver motor court and an arched entry. Inside, beamed ceilings and oversized black-framed windows pull the yard into the living space, and the kitchen runs the length of the great room with a full butler's pantry behind it. Beyond the main living wing there's a wine room, a media room, and a primary suite with a marble bath. Out back, a covered living room with a fireplace looks across the deck to the pool, the pergola, and the lawn.
