Portfolio · Georgetown, TX

Cimarron Hills

A complete backyard transformation — pool, spa, outdoor living, and an outdoor kitchen, built as one.

The project

One backyard, every trade

This project brought the whole Fortis playbook into a single backyard in the Cimarron Hills golf community west of Georgetown. A gunite pool and spa with fountains anchor the space, wrapped in light stone decking. Under the extended roofline, a tongue-and-groove ceiling covers a full outdoor living room — fireplace, TV, and ceiling fans — and the outdoor kitchen pairs a built-in gas grill with a kamado smoker. Designed and built by one team, start to finish.
